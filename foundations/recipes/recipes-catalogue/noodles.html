Stir-Fry Noodles

Photo by ikhsan baihaqi on Unsplash

Description

Stir-fried noodles is a quick and delicious dish packed with colorful vegetables and tender noodles tossed in a savory soy-based sauce.
It's simple, healthy, and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 250g egg noodles (or any instant or spaghetti noodles)
  • 1 medium carrot (julienned)
  • ½ red bell pepper (sliced thinly)
  • ½ green bell pepper (sliced thinly)
  • ½ onion (sliced)
  • 1 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional, for aroma)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
